Project Brief

Emily was approached by Tradiee to create a logo, design their app screens and to design a website promoting the app.

Logo Design

Tradiee wanted to update their existing logo’s colours, shapes and sizes. Emily supplied the newly updated logo along with a style guide presenting brand colours and fonts.

App Design

Emily was responsible for designing the screens for the Tradiee app. The goal for the design was to keep inline with current iOS trends while considering the target audience. Emily also designed email templates and automatically created PDFs. View the Tradiee app in the App Store.

Website Design

The purpose of the website is to promote the app and to inform people on how to download it to their smart phones. The one-page website was kept simple focusing on the main key points. By working across a suite of services, Emily was able to ensure consistency throughout the brand, app and website
